About Me

My name is Zodwa Ndhlovu. I live in Orange Farm, the south of Johannesburg. I matriculated in year 2010. I am a third year education student at the University of Johannesburg, I specialize in isiZulu home language and historical studies. I am a talkative person and friendly at the same time. I like staying indoors, watch television and reading newspapers. I like listening to motivational talks. People who work hard inspire me.
